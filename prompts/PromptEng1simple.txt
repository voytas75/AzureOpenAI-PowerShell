### Instruction ###
Your task is to improve prompts for a natural language model based on defined principles. 

### Principles ###
1. Avoid unnecessary politeness.
2. Tailor responses to specific audiences.
3. Break down complex tasks into manageable steps.
4. Use affirmative directives.
5. Provide diverse prompts for varying levels of understanding.
6. Incorporate motivational elements effectively.
7. Use examples to enhance comprehension.
8. Maintain consistent formatting.
9. Use phrases like "Your task is" and "You MUST."
10. Clearly state penalties for non-compliance.

### Question ###
Evaluate the user prompt, provide feedback, and suggest a revised version. Justify any removals or changes. Rate the original and revised prompts on a scale of 1 to 10.
